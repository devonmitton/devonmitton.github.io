Canadian Tire Innovations

Senior UX Designer, October 2014 — Present

Life in Canada depends on Canadian Tire, which means that Canadian Tire needs to really understand Canadians and what they need, even before they know they need it.

The Canadian Tire Innovations group is the group that is looking forward to the horizon to find the next thing to change the landscape of retail, and what we expect from life in Canada.

Think Shift Advertising

Senior UX Designer, March 2011 — October 2014

It was really good to be at Think Shift. The company specializes in brand, culture and leadership, and the proof is in how great those things are at Think Shift.

I started as an Interaction Designer then changed into the role of User Experience Designer where I worked closely with clients like Cabela's Canada, Cargill Ag Canada and eCampus Manitoba.

Some Projects I've Worked On

Cabela's Canada

59 % increase in sales from same day previous year

Photo Credit: Vladimir Naumoff

I helped Cabela's fine tune their website response times and optimize the experience of shopping and buying.

  • User Tests
  • Performance
  • UI Design
  • I performed usability tests to find opportunities for quick and easy fixes to the website experience.
  • I fine-tuned the performance of the website, reducing load times from eight seconds down to one.
  • I tweaked and optimized the user interface to help people shop and purchase easier.

Visit the Cabela's Canada Website.

Cargill Ag Canada

60 % decrease in visitor bounces

Photo Credit: Susanne Nilsson

I helped Cargill take six websites and combine them into “one website to rule them all” and make it easier for people find what they're looking for.

  • Info Design
  • User Flows
  • Wireframes
  • I restructured six websites into a single website, created content models and copywriting templates.
  • I created a navigation hierarchy that makes it easy for people to find what they're looking for.
  • I collaborated with the team to create wireframes for the interfaces.

Visit the CargillAg Canada website.

eCampus Manitoba

34 % increase in pages visited per session

Photo Credit: AJ Batac

I helped eCampus Manitoba create a website that is easy to understand, so eCampus can make the process of getting an education easy to understand.

  • Info Design
  • User Tests
  • UI Design
  • I structured the website and created content models.
  • I performed usability tests on the website to ensure our efforts were working or not.
  • I designed a revised “Help Centre” which provides website visitors to find information on their own, and reduce the calls into the office.
