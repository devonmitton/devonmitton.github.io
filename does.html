Think Shift Advertising

Senior UX Designer, March 2011 — October 2014

It was really good to be at Think Shift. The company specializes in Brand, Culture and Leadership, and the proof is in how strong all three of them are at Think Shift.

I started there as an Interaction Designer, then changed into the role of User Experience Designer where I worked closely with clients like Cabela's Canada, Cargill Ag and eCampus Manitoba.

Cabela's Canada

I designed and built a new primary navigation system that made use of a “Mega Menu” design pattern. As a result we saw:

59.1 % increase in sales from same day previous year

I joined the Cabela's Team at Think Shift to help optimize the user's experience. One of the first things I did was perform usability tests with audience members to identify quick and easy opportunities for improvement.

The results of the tests showed that the website was a bit sluggish and people were frustrated with the primary navigation.

  • I designed and prototyped the new mega menu system to help people find the right path, and get them to product quicker.
  • I rewrote JavaScript to load the bulk of it after the page was loaded and optimized images and CSS. We reduced load times from eight seconds to one.

Other design work I began for Cabela's included:

  • Redesigning the product details pages to make it easier to choose product options (not yet deployed).
  • Redesigning the product listings pages for better promotions, and preparation for a responsive overhaul (not yet deployed).
  • Redesigning the checkout process to reduce dropoff and increase conversions by simplifying the process (not yet deployed).

CargillAg

CargillAg Canada needed to redesign their website, and amalgamate six separate websites into one single site to rule them all.

I designed the information design to join these sites together. As a result we saw:

60 % decrease in visitor bounces

My role on the project involved:

  • Creating content models for copywriter templates, CMS structure, and content relationships
  • Designing wireframes for website templates
  • Designing user flows for website navigation structure
  • Performing usability testing
  • Creating recommendations for performance and usability optimizations
