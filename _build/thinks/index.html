Speaking

I've always been drawn to teaching. The only problem: I was terrified of public speaking.

In 2014 while working at Think Shift, I decided to get over my fear and start giving talks on Design and User Experience. Since then I have spoken at several “Lunch and Learns”, both to staff and clients, as well as spoken a few times at Red River College to the Graphic Design classes.

Enjoy!
