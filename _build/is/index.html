About Me

What I do, and who I am.

I'm a User Experience Designer

What on earth is that?

A User's Experience is every aspect of a person's interaction with an organization and it's products or services which impact how they think and feel and changes their expectations.

A User Experience Designer is responsible for creating an environment for positive and delightful experiences to happen.

In other words, I like making things easy to understand and easy to use.

What I believe about the work that I do

Not my job, but what I believe my life's work is all about helping people experience better.

There's so much in our world that we use because it's there and it does the job but not really well. I want people to expect more from Design. To expect that it doesn't just solve a problem, but it creates delight.

I hope to try and make the world a better place because the things we use are better. The companies we buy from and work for are better. Our governments are better. Because I've helped change people's perceptions, and they now expect that everything can be better.

It's pretty lofty, I know. However I'm still going to try.
